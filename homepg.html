GoParky

GoParky is a smart vehicle parking management system designed to streamline and simplify the parking process for institutions, businesses, and gated communities. It ensures efficient parking slot allocation, real-time vehicle tracking, and user-friendly access for both admins and staff.

  • Separate dashboards for Admin and Staff users
  • Live tracking and monitoring of parking slots
  • Secure and efficient parking space allocation
  • Scalable for small to large parking areas
  • Professional and intuitive interface design
